Personal Reflection 1: 25 Questions

  1. What is the purpose of what you are currently doing?
  2. Why are you pursuing art?
  3. How do you find purpose and meaning in what you do?
  4. When do you feel the greatest joy when you are working?
  5. What kind of person do you want to be remembered as?
  6. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  7. Where do you want to be after you graduate?
  8. How do you handle transitions and changes in your life?
  9. How does your experiences shape yourself (or your art)?
  10. How do you stay motivationed and passionate to live an active life?
  11. What values and principles guide your life?
  12. What challenges do you face as an artist, and how can you overcome them?
  13. How do you market and promote your creative work?
  14. How do you handle self-doubt and uncertainty?
  15. How do you find your unique and creative voice or style over time?
  16. How do you define personal happiness and fulfillment?
  17. What do you think creativity is and how do you stay creative?
  18. How do you maintain a sense of curiosity and learning throughout life?
  19. What steps can you take to continue growing and evolving?
  20. What is your long-term vision for your life?
  21. How do you make decisions that align with your long-term vision for your life?
  22. What do you think is important for you to learn?
  23. How can you use what you learned in school after you graduate?
  24. How do you stay open-minded?
  25. How do you handle major life decisions and crossroads?
